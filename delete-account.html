Pocket Market: Account Deletion Request

You have the right to request the deletion of your Pocket Market account and all associated data.

To initiate this process, please send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Email: pocketmarkettt@gmail.com

In the body of the email, please include the email address and username associated with the account you wish to delete to help us verify your identity. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws and our data retention policies.

Thank you for using Pocket Market.
